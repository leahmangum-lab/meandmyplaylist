The Power of a Perfect Playlist

Whether you are driving through the mountains, lifting weights, hanging out with friends, or worshipping, there is always a perfect playlist. With the amount of music that exists in the world, it is not hard to find a sing to fit every vibe perfectly. Music can motivate, make you feel emotions deeper, help you relax, and help you feel the Spirit.

My Favorite Artists

Zach Bryan

Zach Bryan is so incredibly good at telling stories in his music and poems. His music is easy to connect to and holds a lot of meaning which is why I love it. He also has a sound that isn't extremely common in country music these days. It's very raw and acoustic.

The Backseat Lovers

The Backseat Lovers have such great music for hanging out with friends, going on drives with the music up, and especially to listen to live. I've heard them three times now and they are incredible. It is very rare to find an artist who sounds the same if not better live.

LANY

Paul Klein writes the most perfect music for any situation. Their album "Soft" is easily a top ten for me right now. Their music is perfect to have on in the background, use to get over a break up, or even to think about someone you love.
